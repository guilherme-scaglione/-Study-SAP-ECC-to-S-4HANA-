## High-level overview

# Accounting
- Financial accounting (FI): general ledger, accounts payable/recievable
- Controlling (CO): profit center and cost center accounting
- Project System (PS): project planning, monitoring & costing

# Logistics
- Materials Management (MM): Purchasing, inventory management and valuation
- Production Planning (PP): order processing, materials requirement planning
- Sales and Distribution (SD) order processing and shipping to customers
- Quality mgmt (QM): Incoming, in-process and final quality inspections
- Plant maintenance (PM): Breakdown, predictive and preventive maintenance functions

# HR
- Human capital management (HCM): entire cycle of employees

## Industry-specific

# Industry solutions (IS)
- Example: Automotive (IS-A), utilities (IS-U)

# Financial services (FS)
- Example: Claims Management (FS-CM), Incentive/comission management (FS-ICM)
