# Benefits

## Performance
- Eliminates information silos (single source of truth)
- Automates core processes (rows to automate orders from supplier when low stock)
- Better decision-making (all data is one system, real time reports) 
- Prioritizes security (seggregation of duties)
- Ensures compliance (auto generates standardized reports)
